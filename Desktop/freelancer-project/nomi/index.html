Building Nomi was founded in 2020 with the ambition to build cool software for awesome organizations. The team have spent a great deal of their careers in self-managed, teal, non-bureaucratic organizations. Our deep love for these organizations inspired us to create software from a human-centered, deliberately developmental mindset, thus enabling all kinds of organizations to work in more humanizing and self-organizing ways.

Our mission is to create the workplaces we would like our children to work in.
